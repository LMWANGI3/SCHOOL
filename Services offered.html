Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Tripple B services?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact our team through the contact form or call us directly. We'll schedule a demo and help you choose the right package for your institution.

Is training provided for teachers and staff?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for all users. Our training includes both initial onboarding sessions and ongoing professional development to ensure your team gets the most out of our platform.

Can the platform integrate with our existing systems?

Our platform is designed to integrate with most common school management systems. During the onboarding process, we'll assess your current systems and ensure a smooth integration.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 technical support through multiple channels including phone, email, and live chat. We also have an extensive knowledge base and regular training webinars.
