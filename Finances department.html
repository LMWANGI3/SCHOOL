Finance Department

Ensuring transparency, accountability, and efficiency in school financial management.

About the Finance Department

The Finance Department is at the heart of our institution's operations, managing all financial resources with integrity and transparency. We are dedicated to providing accurate, timely, and efficient financial services to students, parents, staff, and all stakeholders.

Our goal is to ensure financial sustainability and accountability by implementing sound financial policies, automating school fee processing, and supporting our community with clear, reliable financial information.

Our Core Responsibilities

Budget Management

Preparation and monitoring of annual school budgets to ensure efficient allocation of resources.

Fee Collection

Collection and recording of school fees and other revenues through multiple convenient payment channels.

Payment Processing

Processing payments and managing expenditure efficiently while maintaining accurate records.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensuring compliance with government and institutional financial regulations and standards.

Financial Reporting

Providing accurate financial reports to administration and stakeholders for informed decision-making.

Financial Strategy

Advising management on cost-effective measures and investment strategies for financial sustainability.

M-Pesa Payment

M-Pesa payments offer a fast and convenient way to pay your fees from anywhere in Kenya.

Steps to Pay via M-Pesa:

  1. Go to M-Pesa Menu on your phone.
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa.
  3. Choose Paybill.
  4. Enter Business Number: 456123
  5. Enter Account Number: Student Admission Number.
  6. Enter Amount and confirm.
  7. You will receive an M-Pesa confirmation message.
